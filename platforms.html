OptRL

From rewards to reality.

OptRL is Proviq's real-time learning and decision optimization platform. It is designed for environments where actions matter, outcomes unfold over time, and optimal decisions cannot be hard-coded.

Capabilities

Decision intelligence that keeps learning.

  • Learn from delayed and sparse feedback.
  • Optimize policies under real-world constraints.
  • Adapt continuously as environments change.
  • Maintain decision traces for analysis and control.
Impact

Self-improving decision engines.

OptRL transforms workflows, recommendation systems, and operational pipelines into self-improving decision engines where rewards reflect reality.

TuskerAI

Visual intelligence in your infrastructure.

TuskerAI is a no-code and low-code computer vision platform designed for rapid deployment at scale. It turns cameras and video streams into intelligent sensors.

Capabilities

Real-time perception for real environments.

  • Detect, track, and reason about visual events.
  • Integrate visual insights into existing systems.
  • Improve models continuously through feedback.
  • Operate with traceable and controllable intelligence.
Impact

Actionable intelligence from raw video.

TuskerAI powers vigilance, monitoring, safety, analytics, and automation without disrupting current infrastructure.
